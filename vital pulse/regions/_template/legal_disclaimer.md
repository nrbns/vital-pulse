## 1. Platform Nature & No Medical Service

**Pulse is a community platform** that connects blood donors with people in need. Pulse is **NOT**:
- A medical service provider
- A hospital or blood bank
- A licensed healthcare organization
- A replacement for professional medical care

We only facilitate connections between registered users and medical facilities. We do NOT store, test, transfuse, or handle blood.

---

## 2. No Guarantees

**Pulse CANNOT guarantee:**
- Blood will be available
- Donors will respond to requests
- Blood matches will be successful
- All information provided is accurate or up-to-date
- Donors will arrive on time
- Medical facilities will have capacity

**Always verify blood availability directly with hospitals or blood banks** before relying on any information from this platform.

---

## 3. User Responsibility

**You are solely responsible for:**
- Verifying the legitimacy of any blood request
- Confirming blood availability with hospitals directly
- Ensuring proper medical procedures are followed
- Validating donor information before arranging donations
- Following all local health and safety regulations
- NEVER exchanging money for blood donations on this platform

**Violation of this responsibility may result in immediate account suspension or legal action.**

---

## 4. Mandatory Information Requirements

**Every blood request MUST include:**
1. Hospital name (mandatory)
2. Hospital bed/ward number (mandatory)

Requests without this information will be **rejected automatically** to prevent fake/hoax requests.

---

## 5. Privacy & Data Protection

- Your phone number is **never shown directly** to other users
- All communication happens through masked numbers or in-app messaging
- Location data is used only for matching nearby donors/hospitals
- Personal health information is stored securely and never shared publicly

See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

---

## 6. Auto-Moderation & Safety

**The platform automatically:**
- Hides blood requests after 3 reports of abuse/fake
- Temporarily bans users who create fake requests (24 hours)
- Rate limits: Maximum 3 blood requests per 24 hours per user
- Rate limits: Maximum 3 calls per 24 hours per user

**You must report abuse immediately** using the report button on any request.
